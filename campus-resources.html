Volunteer Opportunities by Campus

University-Wide

  • CUNY Citizenship Now! – Support immigration legal aid and outreach events across all campuses.
  • CUNY Volunteer Corps – Volunteer at city-wide events open to all CUNY students.
  • Macaulay Honors College – Required service for Macaulay students across all affiliated campuses.

Queens College

  • Knights Day of Service – Volunteer at local shelters, parks, and food pantries.
  • Food Pantry & Clothing Drives – Ongoing donation-based efforts on campus.

Hunter College

  • Hunter Has Heart – Partners with NYC Cares and organizes campus-wide drives.

Hostos Community College

  • Hostos Volunteer Corps – Coordinates outreach projects in the South Bronx.
  • Community Garden – Student-run food justice initiative on campus.

Baruch College

  • Food Drive Cleanup – Hosted by student government and volunteer clubs.

City College of New York

  • Environmental Cleanup Days – Student-led environmental volunteering around Harlem.

Brooklyn College

  • Voter Registration Drives – Civic engagement initiatives led by student orgs.

John Jay College

  • Community Justice Projects – Volunteering aligned with social justice and legal outreach.

Lehman College

  • Lehman L.O.V.E.S. – Sustainability and community volunteer efforts organized on campus.

Other Campuses

  • CSI, Medgar Evers, York, City Tech – Various student-club-led cleanups, drives, and volunteer events without academic incentives.
